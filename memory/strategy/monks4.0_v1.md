## Brand Identity

Monks 4.0 represents the evolution from digital agency to AI-first transformation partner. The brand signals technical depth, strategic authority, and operational excellence.

### Visual System
- **Primary palette:** Deep navy/black (#0a0a0f), Electric purple (#6c5ce7), Soft lavender (#a29bfe)
- **Accent colors:** Teal (#00cec9) for success/active states, Gold (#fdcb6e) for pins/warnings
- **Typography:** Inter (primary), system sans-serif (fallback)
- **Dark theme first:** All digital artifacts default to dark mode

### Tone of Voice
- Challenger-minded: We reframe problems, not recite solutions
- Zero-fluff: If it doesn't scale or drive value, we don't say it
- C-suite direct: Language for decision-makers, not committees
