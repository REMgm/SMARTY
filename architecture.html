1 Design Principles

Outcome-Obsessed

Every component maps to a business KPI. If it doesn't drive value, it's out.

Composable by Default

API-first across Adobe, Salesforce, and AWS. No monoliths, no vendor lock-in.

Agentic AI at Core

Autonomous workflows for content, media, campaigns, and real-time personalization.

Flip-Thinking Native

Every assumption gets challenged. Architecture designed to be reframed from multiple angles.

Cumulative Intelligence

Every deployment, every data point feeds back. Nothing is wasted.

2 Architecture Layers

Foundation

Layer 1 — Data Orchestration

Real-time data ingestion and unification. CDP integration (Adobe Real-Time CDP / Salesforce Data Cloud), event streaming (AWS Kinesis / EventBridge), and identity resolution.

Intelligence

Layer 2 — AI Engine

Agentic AI workflows: LLM content pipelines, predictive analytics, automated A/B testing, and personalization engines.

Execution

Layer 3 — Experience Delivery

Composable CX: headless CMS, commerce, marketing automation. Adobe Experience Manager, Salesforce Marketing Cloud, custom micro-frontends.

Feedback Loop

Layer 4 — Measurement & Optimization

Attribution modeling, real-time dashboards, continuous optimization. Every touchpoint feeds back into Layer 1.
